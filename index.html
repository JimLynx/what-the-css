ABOUT THIS SITE

You are NOT alone!

Pretty much everyone struggles with Javascript and it's certainly not an easy language to learn.

Seasoned professional programmers STILL use Google and external resources to solve issues and jog the memory of what to use where... but with more practice, the more fluid it becomes.

Here are some tips to help your learning curve:

  • - Learn bite-sized chunks at a time
  • - Don't try and remember everything, you can't.
  • - Supplement learning with small courses
  • - Take part in challenges to practice your coding

Online Courses

There are some amazing free courses available online!

Remember that these courses are aimed at supplementing your learning, so don't expect to know all of Javascript immediately!
