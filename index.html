ABOUT THIS SITE

You are NOT alone!

CSS or "Cascading Style Sheets" can be a little confusing at first, but once you get the hang of it, it's awesome to play with!

In this site, checkout some of the Tutorials, additional supplementary Courses and even a few Games to help you get your CSS brain cranking

Here are some tips to help your learning curve:

  • - Learn bite-sized chunks at a time
  • - Don't try and remember everything, just take one step at a time.
  • - Supplement learning with small courses
  • - Only use WHAT you want WHEN you need it... it's truly the only way to go! You'll pick up new tips along the way each time you start coding CSS in your project.
